About

Media at the
intersection of humanity

Roman Levingard is an international digital media creator, social entrepreneur, and community storyteller based in Haifa, Israel. Over more than two decades, he has built one of Israel's most expansive independent media networks — spanning 100+ social media pages, 20+ YouTube channels, and a multilingual audience across Israel, Ukraine, Russia, and the United States.

What distinguishes Roman's work is not scale alone, but purpose. Every channel, every video, every community page is built around a belief that underserved people — immigrant communities, small business owners, the elderly, those navigating mental health challenges — deserve professional-quality storytelling and visibility.

As an early adopter of AI-driven content production, Roman pioneered a model that compresses agency-level media capacity into a one-person operation — enabling him to provide free or subsidized digital services to 50+ nonprofits and small businesses in Israel.

He works at the unique intersection of digital media, mental health advocacy, and social welfare — a cross-disciplinary position that has made him a sought-after collaborator for community organizations and cultural institutions across multiple countries.
